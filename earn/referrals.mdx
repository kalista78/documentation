## Referral Mathematics

### For Your Friends
When they earn points through any activity, they get a **10% bonus**:
- Base points: 100
- With your referral: 110 points (100 + 10% bonus)

### For You
You earn **25% of their base points** (not including their bonus):
- Friend's base points: 100
- You receive: 25 points

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do referral points count toward my tier?">
    Yes! All points (trading, creator, and referral) count toward your tier ranking.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I refer someone after they've already signed up?">
    No, referral links must be used during initial sign-up to establish the connection.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I get points from my referrals' referrals?">
    No, the referral system is one level deep. You only earn from direct referrals.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to referral earnings?">
    No limits! Refer as many people as you want and earn from all of them.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
