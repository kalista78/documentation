## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When can I withdraw fees?">
    Anytime! There's no minimum amount or waiting period. Fees are yours immediately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What currency are fees paid in?">
    Fees are collected in WHYPE (Wrapped HYPE), the native currency of HyperEVM.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do fees ever expire?">
    Never! Your fees accumulate forever. Even if you don't claim for years, they'll still be there.
  </Accordion>
