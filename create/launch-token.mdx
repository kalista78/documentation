## Before You Launch

### What You Need
- **Token Name**: Be creative and memorable
- **Symbol**: 3-10 characters (check it's not taken)
- **Logo** (optional but recommended): Upload an image or provide URL
- **Description** (optional): Sell your vision to potential holders

### Important Limits
<Warning>
  You can create up to **2 tokens per platform every 24 hours**
</Warning>

This means you could potentially launch:
- 2 tokens via Farcaster
- 2 tokens via Twitter
- 2 tokens via Web App
- **Total**: Up to 6 tokens per day across all platforms
