## Method 1: Direct Cast

### How It Works

Simply tag @zyperbot in a cast with your token details:

```
@zyperbot launch "Token Name" $SYMBOL
logo: https://your-image-url.com/logo.png
```

The bot instantly deploys your token and replies with all the details!

## Method 2: Mini App

### Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Farcaster">
    Launch Farcaster on mobile or desktop
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Zyper Mini App">
    Look for the Zyper mini app in the app drawer or search "Zyper"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    The app automatically uses your Farcaster-connected wallet
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Token Details">
    - Enter token name
    - Choose symbol
    - Upload logo (optional)
    - Add description (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch">
    Click "Launch Token" and confirm - no gas fees!
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Share">
    The app creates a cast announcing your token launch
  </Step>
</Steps>
