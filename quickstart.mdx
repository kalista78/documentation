## Understanding Your Earnings

Every token on Zyper has a **1% trading fee**:
- **0.5% goes to YOU** (the creator)
- **0.5% goes to Zyper** (platform fee)

### Real Examples (in HYPE)

| If someone trades... | Platform gets | YOU get |
|---------------------|---------------|---------|
| 100 HYPE | 0.5 HYPE | **0.5 HYPE** |
| 1,000 HYPE | 5 HYPE | **5 HYPE** |
| 10,000 HYPE | 50 HYPE | **50 HYPE** |
| 100,000 HYPE | 500 HYPE | **500 HYPE** |

## Points System

### Earn Points Two Ways:

- **As a Creator**: Get **400 points** for every 1 WHYPE you collect in fees
- **As a Trader**: Get **1 point** for every 1 WHYPE trading volume

Example: If you collect 10 WHYPE in fees today, you earn 4,000 points!
