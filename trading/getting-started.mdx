## Trading on zyper.fun

### Sign In with Privy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit zyper.fun">
    Go to [zyper.fun](https://zyper.fun) in your browser
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Sign In">
    Choose your preferred sign-in method:
    - **Farcaster** (recommended for seamless experience)
    - **Twitter** (use your Twitter account)
    - **External Wallet** (MetaMask, Rainbow, etc.)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Authentication">
    Follow the prompts to authenticate with your chosen method
  </Step>
</Steps>
